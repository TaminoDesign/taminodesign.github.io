About me:

I'm a 15-year-old Homeschooled teen who loves creating, designing and building websites.
I also really like to read (anything fiction really).
My website building started when my dad wanted to start his website where he sells online courses for kids and teens (YoungCre8ors.com), so we (my dad and I) looked at different website-building options.
We tried at least three or four different ones, but we found that all of them were not what we were looking for (they all lacked customisation).
My dad didn't have time to look at another option, so I said I'd look at WordPress for him, and I ended up building him a WordPress website.
Since then I noticed how much I liked building websites, and have built two more (the second website I built was Getworldschooling.com.
The third one was completely redoing my first website).
I also really enjoy graphic design which is great because I can combine the two and design graphics for the websites I build.
