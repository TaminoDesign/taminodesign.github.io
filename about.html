About me

I'm a 16-year-old Homeschooled teen who loves creating, designing and building websites. I also really like to read anything fiction really.

My website building started when my dad wanted to start his website where he sells online courses for kids and teens YoungCre8ors.com, so my dad and I looked at different website-building options.
We tried at least three or four different ones, but we found that all of them were not what we were looking for (thay all lacked customisation.)

My dad didn't have time to look at another option, so I said I'd look at WordPress for him, and I ended up building him a WordPress site. I noticed how much I liked building websites, and have built a few more since then.

I also really enjoy graphic design which is great because I can combine the two and design graphics for the websites I build.
